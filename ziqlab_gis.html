Wadi Ziqlab GIS data  post.
  This download area is intended for members of the University of Toronto Wadi Ziqlab Project and the ASU SHESC Mediterranean Landscape Dynamics Project. If you are not affiliated with either of these two projects, please e-mail me for permission before downloading or using these datasets in any way. 
  Current links below include the zipped archive of GeoTIFF rasters for the area, and a zipped archive of the shapefile I made from the survey database information. Both the rasters and the shapefile have the same projection: WGS84 UTM Zone N36. Note that the shapefile has a slightly larger extent than the geotiffs as I arbitrarily sized the DEM before I had knew the extent of the survey boundaries. As of now, a few sites appear off the edge of the DEM. I will make a new DEM and put it up soon so that the entirety of the survey data can be overlayed on it.
   As newer datasets become available, I will have to take down older files to make space. I will keep a list of all the previously available files below. If you need a file that is no longer available, e-mail me, and I will re-post it.
